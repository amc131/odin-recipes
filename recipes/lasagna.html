World's Best Lasagna

Make allrecipes.com most popular lasagna today and find out what all the fuss is about!

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

This lasagna recipe is incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, these are the ingredients you'll need to add:

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
