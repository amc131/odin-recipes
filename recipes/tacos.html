Chorizo Tacos

Easy Chorizo Street Tacos

Amazing tacos only needing 5 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 chorizo sausage link, casing removed and meat crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion, or to taste
  • fresh cilantro to taste

Steps

  1. Mix chorizo and chipotle peppers in adobo sauce together in a bowl.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat; add chorizo mixture and cook until crisp, 5-7 minutes. Transfer chorizo to a plate, reserving grease in the skillet.
  3. Heat tortillas in the reserved grease in skillet over medium heat until warmed, 1-2 minutes per side. Stack 2 tortillas on top of each other for each taco. Fill with chorizo, onion, and cilantro.
