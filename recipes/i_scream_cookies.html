Festive Scream Cookies

I used chocolate to make a screaming ghost face design on these Halloween-themed ice cream cookie sandwiches just so I could do a play on words.

Ingredients

  • 8 large chocolate chip cookies
  • 3 tablespoons melted dark chocolate
  • 4 teaspoons melted white chocolate
  • 1/2 cup vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Cover the flat side of 4 cookies with melted dark chocolate; tap the cookies lightly to smooth out the chocolate.
  2. While still wet, use the piping bag or spoon to make a ghost shape with the white chocolate; tap lightly once more to smooth as needed. More white designs can be added to the dark chocolate background if desired.
  3. Use the melted dark chocolate to make the eyes and screaming mouth; tap or shake once more to even and smooth out.
  4. Let chocolate cool until hard and dry to the touch, at least 1 hour.
  5. Scoop the ice cream on 4 remaining cookies, and cover with the tops. Carefully press to flatten, and transfer to freeze thoroughly before serving.
