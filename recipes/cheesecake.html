New York Cheesecake

A classic New York-style cheesecake with a graham cracker crust and creamy, lightly flavored vanilla center. The cream cheese mixture also has sour cream for an extra smooth and creamy texture.

Ingredients

  • 15 graham crackers, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
